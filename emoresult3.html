You Might Be Depressed or Sad




It is normal for us to have bad moods and bad days, even bad weeks. However, it is important

that we are able to release the bad energy built up in our bodies so that we can move on.

We understand that you do have someone to speak to regarding your issues, so we recommend reaching

out to them. Remember, a bad day, week or even month does not mean a bad life. You got this!
