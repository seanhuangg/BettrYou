You Might Need to Assess Your Situation




Losing interest in things you're normally interested in is definitely something to pay
attention to, as it may be the first warning sign of developing depression.
When faced with this challenge, it is important to ask yourself a few 'Why" questions.



1. Why do I not feel the same anymore? Is it the people that I'm around? Is it because I am not happy with myself?

2. Why did I pursue this hobby/pastime in the first place? Is it because it brought me something?

3. Why would other people lose their interest in this hobby, and how can I pick myself back up?
