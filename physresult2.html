You Might Have Some Dietary Imbalances




You have your workout routine in check. Great. However, it is just important to recover as

it is to workout. Since your energy levels may have seen a slight decrease, we recommend that you

incorporate red meat into your diet. Red meat(beef, lamb, etc.) has essential minerals and boosts

Sleep quality. If you are a vegetarian or vegan, we recommend plant-based protein sources such as

organic tofu, black beans, and raw spinach. Happy eating!
